about

Posted on Jun 23, 2026

Born and raised in Siberia, in (arguably) the most beautiful town in the world Tomsk. I lived in Moscow (Russia), Tromsø (Norway), and Lewisburg (PA, USA) for a while before coming to Columbus, OH to do my PhD. In my free time, I enjoy crocheting and walking around with no particular purpose. I have been a huge fan of the Eurovision Song Contest for already 13 years.
